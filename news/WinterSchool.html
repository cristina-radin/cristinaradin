AI for Earth System, Hazards & Climate Extremes

Inspiring talks, exciting group team challenges, connecting with researchers and exchanging ideas... that and a lot more happened last week in the ELLIS Winter School on “AI for Earth System, Hazards & Climate Extremes” at the National Technical University of Athens (March 16–20, 2026) 🌍. It was truly amazing to discuss where the AI community focused on extreme events in our climate is moving towards: our challenges and our strengths, talking about interpretability and trustworthiness, and translating the words into practical exercises. Now it's time to step back, absorb it all, and bring it to life.

Many thanks to everyone involved in the organization! 👏
